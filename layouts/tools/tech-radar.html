Quadrants

  • Techniques - Methods and practices for software development.

Rings

  • Authority field - Confident teaching/leading/automating with these.
  • Good knowledge - Actively use and can troubleshoot in most scenarios.
  • Some knowledge - Used in projects, but may need docs or further study.
  • No knowledge - Heard of or briefly seen, but not used hands-on.
  • No interest - Deliberately not interested in using/learning.
