azure: [Launched] Generally Available: Azure Dl/D/E v7 Virtual Machines

May 7, 2026

Azure has made Dl/D/E v7 virtual machines generally available. The new general-purpose and memory-optimized VMs use Intel Xeon 6 (Granite Rapids) processors and claim up to 20% better general compute performance.
