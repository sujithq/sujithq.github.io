Azure Content Understanding in Foundry Tools is described as a comprehensive content AI service for ingesting documents, audio, images, and video and extracting key information for grounded generative AI and agentic solutions. It combines Azure Document Intelligence’s traditional AI with LLM-based content capabilities.
