Microsoft Defender researchers reported a resurgence of a multi-stage adversary-in-the-middle (AiTM) phishing campaign that evolved into business email compromise (BEC) attacks by abusing SharePoint. The campaign targeted multiple organizations in the energy sector, and Microsoft published details and guidance on the Microsoft Security Blog.
