May 2026 Microsoft Foundry updates include trace-based evaluation for any agent on any cloud, new catalog models (Grok 4.3 and DeepSeek V4), GPT-5 Reinforcement Fine-Tuning at gated GA, and several platform and SDK additions. The post also notes GA for Managed VNET and Content Understanding improvements, plus Foundry Local updates and a new azure-ai-projects release.
