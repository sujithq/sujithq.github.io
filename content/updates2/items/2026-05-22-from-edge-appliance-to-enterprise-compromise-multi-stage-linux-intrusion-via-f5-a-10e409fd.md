Microsoft describes a multi-stage Linux intrusion that started from an exposed F5 BIG-IP appliance, then pivoted to an internal Confluence server to steal credentials and compromise identity. The attack involved attempted Kerberos relay and lateral movement, with Microsoft Defender detecting and blocking parts of the activity.
