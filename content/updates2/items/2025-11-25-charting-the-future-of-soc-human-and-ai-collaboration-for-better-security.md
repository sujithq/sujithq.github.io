Microsoft describes its experience building autonomous AI agents for Managed Detection and Response (MDR) and examines how generative AI is transforming Security Operations Centers (SOCs). The post highlights a shift toward GenAI-powered SOCs that emphasize collaborative workflows where AI augments analysts, automates routine tasks, and requires human oversight, governance, and continual learning to improve security outcomes.
