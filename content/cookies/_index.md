> This policy covers the root domain quintelier.dev only. Application subdomains are operated separately.

## What we use

- **Essential delivery:** Static pages are served from GitHub Pages with Cloudflare caching. No advertising, marketing, or behavioural profiling cookies are set.
- **Preferences:** Theme choice is stored locally to remember your light/dark preference.
- **Analytics (opt-in):** Microsoft Clarity loads only after you accept via the banner. Declining prevents Clarity from loading.

## Microsoft Clarity (optional)

- **Purpose:** Understand how readers use the site (clicks, scroll depth, device type) to improve content and accessibility.
- **Data:** Page URLs, referrers, interaction events, device/viewport details, and consent status. No payment data or authentication flows exist on this domain.
- **Legal basis:** Your consent. Without consent, Clarity is not loaded.
- **Where it runs:** Scripts are served from clarity.ms. Data may be processed in the EU or US under the provider’s safeguards.
