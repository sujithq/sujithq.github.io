## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They are used to remember preferences, enable site functionality, and (with consent) support analytics.

## Cookies used on this site

### Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are required for the site to function correctly. They do not track you or collect personal data. No consent is required for these.

| Name | Purpose | Duration |
|------|---------|----------|
| `theme` | Stores your light/dark theme preference | Browser session / localStorage |
| `clarityConsent` | Records whether you have accepted or declined analytics cookies | Browser session / localStorage |

### Analytics cookies (consent required)

These cookies are only set if you **accept** via the cookie consent banner. Without consent, no analytics cookies are placed.

| Name | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|------|----------|---------|----------|
| `_clck` | Microsoft Clarity | Persists user ID and preferences | 1 year |
| `_clsk` | Microsoft Clarity | Connects multiple page views in a single session | 1 day |
| `CLID` | Microsoft Clarity | Identifies the first time Clarity saw this user | 1 year |
| `ANONCHK` | Microsoft Clarity | Session check | 10 minutes |
| `MR` | Microsoft Clarity | Indicates whether to refresh `MUID` | 7 days |
| `MUID` | Microsoft Clarity | Identifies unique web browsers visiting Microsoft sites | 1 year |
| `SM` | Microsoft Clarity | Synchronises the MUID across Microsoft domains | Session |

For full details on Microsoft Clarity's data handling, see [Microsoft's Privacy Statement](https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/privacystatement).

## Consent model

When you first visit this site, a banner appears asking for your consent to use analytics cookies (Microsoft Clarity). You can:

- **Accept**: Analytics cookies are enabled and Clarity begins recording session data.
- **Decline**: No analytics cookies are placed. Clarity is not loaded.

You can change your mind at any time using the reset link below or on the [Privacy Policy](/privacy/#manage-your-consent) page.

## Managing cookies in your browser

You can also control cookies directly in your browser settings:

- **Chrome**: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox**: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari**: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
- **Edge**: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Note that disabling all cookies may affect site functionality (for example, theme preferences will not be saved).
