## Important Security Notice

### Verify Communication Authenticity

If you receive any communication claiming to be from me:

1. **Verify the sender email domain** - I only use `@gmail.com` for personal correspondence related to this site
2. **Check digital signatures** - Legitimate messages from me may include verifiable identity markers
3. **Be cautious of requests** - I will never ask for passwords, credentials, or sensitive financial information via email

If you're unsure whether a message is genuine, reach out to me directly at the email address listed above to verify.
