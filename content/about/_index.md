## About quintelier.dev

This is the personal technical blog of **Sujith Quintelier**, hosted at **quintelier.dev**. This site serves as a platform to share knowledge, insights, and updates on cloud technologies, DevOps practices, and software development.

## Who I Am

I'm **Sujith Quintelier**, an Azure and GitHub consultant with over 25 years of experience in IT. I specialise in:

- **Cloud Architecture** with a focus on Microsoft Azure
- **DevOps** practices and automation
- **GitHub** training and consulting
- **Platform Engineering** and developer experience
- **Security** and compliance in cloud environments
- **AI and Machine Learning** solutions

## What This Site Is For

quintelier.dev is a personal blog where I share:

- **Technical tutorials** and guides on Azure, GitHub, Terraform, and related technologies
- **Best practices** for DevOps, security, and cloud architecture
- **Updates** on the latest developments in cloud and DevOps
- **Insights** from my consulting and training work
- **Tools** and utilities for developers and cloud engineers

## Purpose and Scope

This site is:

- A personal blog, not affiliated with or endorsed by any employer or client
- Focused on education and knowledge sharing
- Updated regularly with new posts and weekly update digests
- Hosted on GitHub Pages and served via Cloudflare
