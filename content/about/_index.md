## Who I am

This website, **quintelier.dev**, is my personal technical blog. I write about Azure, cloud architecture, DevOps practices, GitHub, automation, and related topics. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by any employer or client.

All content reflects my own views and experiences, not those of any organisation I work for or with.
